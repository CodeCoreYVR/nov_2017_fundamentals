Gramma's Chocolate Chip Recipe ©

Ingredients

  • sugar
  • chocolate chips
  • eggs
  • flour
  • baking powder
  • milk
  • salt
  • butter
  • yeast

Instruction

  1. Mix the eggs in the middle of a flour pit
  2. Need the cookie dough until soft
  3. Add chocolate chips
  4. Roll into small balls and place on a baking sheet
  5. Throw it in the oven for 15 minutes at 375°F
